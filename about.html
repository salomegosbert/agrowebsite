About us

About US

AgroSupport is an initiative formed by young Tanzanian women in 2018. The aim being to support young and empower women in the Tanzanian society who are involved in agriculture especially those found in rural areas.

Vision

Building confidence and competence in the market

To connect women in agriculture with agricultural professional

Mision

Improve capacity building in their agriculture activities.

Improve agricultural skills and create awareness among young women

Services

We offer a wide range of services

Market

Our service ensures that the woman farmers makes the most profit out of her efforts incurred in the entire farming process.We offer a direct link betweeen the woman farme in Morogoro and Pwani and her potential market.

Agricultural Professionals

Our service ensures that the woman farmers ha ve access to agricultural experts from all over the country. This is to enable the women farmers to also acquire new farming practises and improve thier quality of farming.

Business advice

We enable the women farmer to be regularly updated on the best practises on how to grow her business. We link the women farmers with other well established women famers for advice and guidance as well as expose them to various agricultural expos where they can benefit from.

Agricultural equipments

We also help the woman farmer when it comes to access ing the most updated and affordable agricultural equipments. We advise and connect them with supplier of such equipments located nearest to the farmer's location.
